How to Take Your Measurements

Watch the video below for a step-by-step guide. Use a soft measuring tape and measure over light clothing, or have someone help you for accuracy.

Need help? Chat with us on WhatsApp.

Quick reference

1

Chest / Bust

Measure around the fullest part of the chest, keeping the tape horizontal and snug but not tight.

2

Waist

Measure around the natural waistline (the narrowest part of the torso, usually just above the navel).

3

Hip

Measure around the fullest part of the hip, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.

4

Shoulder width

Measure from the edge of one shoulder to the edge of the other, across the back.

5

Sleeve length

From the centre back of the neck, over the shoulder and down the arm to the wrist bone.

6

Inseam

From the crotch seam down the inside of the leg to the desired length (e.g. top of shoe or ankle).

Tips for accurate measurements

  • Use a soft, flexible measuring tape.
  • Stand naturally; don’t suck in or puff out.
  • Keep the tape snug but not tight.
  • Measure over thin clothing or underwear for consistency.
  • If possible, have someone else take the measurements.
