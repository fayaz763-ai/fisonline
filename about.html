About FIS Online

Bridging ready-to-wear e-commerce with the personal touch of bespoke tailoring.

Our Story

FIS Online (First | Sartorials) is a modern fashion and lifestyle brand that bridges the gap between ready-to-wear e-commerce and customized tailoring. We focus on men's, women's, and kids' wear, along with footwear, accessories, and lifestyle products.

Our mission is to combine the ease of online shopping with the personal touch of bespoke tailoring — creating a balanced experience of style, comfort, and individuality.

What We Do

We operate through a hybrid model: E-commerce for ready-to-wear and lifestyle with worldwide shipping, and Custom Tailoring where you choose fabrics online, fill in measurement forms with our visual guide, and we offer appointments and doorstep tailoring in Hyderabad (subject to MOQ).

Our Values

  • Style – Curated, contemporary fashion
  • Comfort – Quality materials and fit
  • Individuality – Made to measure when you want it
